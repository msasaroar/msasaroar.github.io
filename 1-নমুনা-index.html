Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

Google Analytics 4 (GA4) is Google’s latest analytics platform designed to provide deeper insights and greater flexibility in understanding user behavior across websites and apps. Unlike its predecessor, Universal Analytics, GA4 adopts an event-based model that allows for more granular tracking of user interactions and provides a holistic view of the customer journey across different devices and platforms. It empowers businesses with advanced features like real-time reporting, cross-platform tracking, and predictive insights that help forecast user behavior trends. By using GA4, companies can track custom events, optimize marketing efforts, and make data-driven decisions that enhance user experience and business outcomes.

Google Tag Manager (GTM)

Google Tag Manager (GTM) is a powerful tag management system that simplifies the process of adding, managing, and controlling tracking codes (tags) across websites and apps without requiring direct code modification. GTM enables marketers, analysts, and developers to implement tags for various purposes, such as tracking custom events, user interactions, conversions, and third-party integrations, without needing constant developer support. With GTM, businesses can quickly deploy and test marketing or analytics tags, ensuring accurate data collection and faster response times to new insights. This flexibility helps teams focus on optimizing their analytics setup, improving the accuracy of data, and enhancing overall site performance.

To prepare for real-time projects with Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Google Tag Manager (GTM), it’s helpful to work on websites with various features like e-commerce, blogs, and landing pages to get a feel for tracking different types of interactions. To add the GA4 Google Merchandise Store demo account to your Google Analytics account, follow these steps: Log In to Google Analytics: Go to Google Analytics and log in with your Google account. Access the Demo Account Link: Visit the official Google Analytics Demo Account link. This page includes details about the demo account and a button to "Access Demo Account". Add the Demo Account: Click on "Access Demo Account". This will automatically add the demo account, which includes both Universal Analytics and GA4 properties for the Google Merchandise Store and the Flood-It mobile app. Navigate to the GA4 Property: Once the demo account is added, go to Admin in Google Analytics. Select the "Google Merchandise Store (GA4)" property under the Account section. Now, you can explore the GA4 property for the Google Merchandise Store. This includes real-time and historical data, giving you hands-on practice with various GA4 features, events, and reporting tools.
