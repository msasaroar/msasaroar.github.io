The top 10 most commonly used eCommerce events for X (Twitter) Ads Pixel are:

AddPaymentInfo – Fires when a user adds or submits payment details.
AddToCart – Triggered when a user adds a product to the cart.
InitiateCheckout – Triggered when a user starts the checkout process.
Lead – Used when a user expresses interest, such as filling out a form.
PageView – Triggered when a user visits a page on your site.
Purchase – Triggered when a user completes a purchase.
RemoveFromCart – Fires when a user removes an item from their cart.
SignUp – Fires when a user creates a new account on the site.
Subscribe – Triggered when a user subscribes to a service or product.
ViewContent – Fires when a user views a product page or important content.
