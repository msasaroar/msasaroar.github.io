Enhanced Measurement Event | outbound_click

Outbound link clicks are links that navigate a user away from your domain to a different website (e.g., Facebook, external blog, partner site).

In Google Analytics 4 (GA4), these clicks are tracked automatically as an Enhanced Measurement event, named:

click event
with outbound = true in the event parameters

Clicking the link below will fire a click event with outbound=true.

Check GA4 DebugView or Realtime reports to confirm the event.
