Why Is Repository Video Engagement Tracking a Custom Event in GTM?

1. Repository videos (such as self-hosted files or embedded from non-YouTube sources) are not automatically tracked by GTM or GA4.

2. GTM does not offer built-in triggers for play, pause, or completion of custom video players.

3. We use a Custom HTML Tag that runs JavaScript to detect video interactions.

4. These scripts push events to dataLayer, and Custom Event Triggers in GTM respond to them.
