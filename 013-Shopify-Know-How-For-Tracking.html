Know How For Tracking On Shopify

What other tasks, besides placing tracking scripts in the header and footer, should I know about?

Key Areas:

Installing GTM and GA4 on Shopify:

  • Use Shopify’s settings or themes to add GTM and GA4 tracking scripts.
  • Shopify Plus users can customize checkout tracking; standard plans have limited options.

E-commerce Tracking:

  • Enhanced E-commerce: Track add-to-cart, product views, purchases, checkout steps, and refunds.
  • Set up GA4 e-commerce events using Shopify’s default or customized data layers.

Custom Event Tracking:

  • Button clicks (e.g., "Buy Now").
  • Product filtering and search.
  • Social sharing events.

Third-Party Apps:

  • Shopify apps (e.g., "Google & YouTube" integration) simplify GA4, GTM, and conversion tracking setups.

Challenges:

  • Limited checkout customizations for non-Shopify Plus users.
  • Reliance on Shopify's data layer for accurate tracking.
